# Children and Health Claims

## Age Stance

Lockd is not intended for children under 13 and should not be submitted in Apple's Kids Category for v1.

## Child Privacy Rules

If Lockd later targets children, schools, family monitoring, parental controls, or child accounts, complete a dedicated review for COPPA, App Store Kids Category rules, parental consent, data minimization, advertising restrictions, and child-directed support flows.

## Health Claim Rules

Lockd should be described as a productivity, focus, and intentional device-use tool. It should not claim to diagnose, treat, cure, prevent, mitigate, or monitor medical or mental health conditions.

## Copy Guidance

Acceptable: "Reduce distractions during study blocks" and "Build more intentional screen time habits."

Avoid: claims that Lockd treats addiction, ADHD, anxiety, depression, sleep disorders, or any clinical condition.

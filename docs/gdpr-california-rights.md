# GDPR and California Privacy Rights

This note maps the current local-first Lockd design to common privacy-rights obligations.

## GDPR

Potential rights include access, deletion, correction, portability, objection, restriction, and complaint to a supervisory authority. The public release must identify the controller, contact method, lawful bases, processors, international transfer safeguards, and retention periods if server-side data is added.

## California

Potential rights include knowing what personal information is collected, deleting personal information, correcting inaccurate personal information, opting out of sale or sharing, limiting certain sensitive information use, and non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights.

## Current V1 Position

Lockd v1 is designed so focus data remains on device. Lockd does not sell personal information, share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising, or use third-party analytics in the current scaffold.

## Operational Requirement

If the product adds accounts, cloud sync, support tickets, email marketing, analytics, crash reporting, or server-based notifications, the team must update the privacy data map, vendor register, Privacy Policy, App Store privacy label, and request workflow.

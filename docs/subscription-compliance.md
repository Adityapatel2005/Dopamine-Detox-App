# Subscription Compliance

Lockd Pro must use Apple In-App Purchase for paid digital features on iOS.

## Required Paywall Disclosures

- Product name.
- Price.
- Billing period.
- Trial length, if any.
- Auto-renewal behavior.
- What features unlock.
- Restore purchases action.
- Link to Privacy Policy.
- Link to Terms of Service.
- Cancellation instructions through Apple subscription settings.

## Copy Guardrails

Do not imply guaranteed productivity, health outcomes, or medical benefits. Do not hide pricing, renewal timing, or cancellation terms. Do not gate core legal links behind purchase.

## Evidence

Keep screenshots of the paywall, purchase screen, restore flow, subscription management link, App Store product setup, and review notes for every release.
